## Run Steps

1. Customer logs in.
2. Customer posts one realistic task.
3. Provider logs in.
4. Provider confirms approval status.
5. Provider applies to the customer task.
6. Customer opens Manage Task.
7. Customer reviews the application.
8. Customer accepts the provider.
9. Customer and provider both open Messages.
10. Customer marks payment status.
11. Customer completes the task.
12. Customer leaves a review.
13. Owner/admin scans Data Management and checks Pilot Run.

## Observe

- Did the customer know where to go after posting?
- Did the provider understand approval and application status?
- Did Manage Task feel clear?
- Did the message window open where expected?
- Did the customer understand when to complete the task?
- Did the review step feel natural?
