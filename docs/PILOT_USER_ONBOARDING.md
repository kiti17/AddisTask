# AddisTask Pilot User Onboarding

Use this when guiding the first small group of customers and providers.

For invitation and follow-up wording, use `docs/PILOT_COMMUNICATION_TEMPLATES.md`.

## Customer Instructions

1. Create an account with your name, phone number, and password.
2. Post one real task you would actually want help with.
3. Choose the service category and Addis Ababa area carefully.
4. Do not put your exact private address in the first task description.
5. Open My Posted Tasks.
6. Open Manage Task.
7. Review provider applications.
8. Accept one provider.
9. Use Messages after the provider is accepted.
10. Mark the task completed only after the work is done.
11. Leave a review.

## Provider Instructions

1. Create an account with your name, phone number, and password.
2. Open Become a Provider.
3. Complete the provider profile with service category, service area, availability, experience, and contact phone.
4. Wait for admin approval.
5. After approval, browse matching open tasks.
6. Apply only to tasks you can actually complete.
7. Check whether applications are pending, accepted, or rejected.
8. Use Messages after a customer accepts you.

## Account Instructions

- Keep your password private.
- If you are logged in and need to change your password, open Account and use Update Password.
- If you enter the wrong password too many times, wait before trying again.
- If you forget your password during the pilot, contact the owner directly.

## What To Observe

- Did the user know which button to click next?
- Did the user scroll up and down looking for something?
- Did the customer understand Manage Task?
- Did the provider understand approval status?
- Did messages appear only after acceptance?
- Did any wording feel confusing?
