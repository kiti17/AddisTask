# AddisTask Pilot Communication Templates

Use these messages for the first controlled pilot. Keep the group small and personal.

## When To Send Messages

- Send customer invitations when there are not enough realistic open tasks.
- Send provider invitations when fewer than 2 trusted providers are ready.
- Send provider profile reminders when profiles are waiting or incomplete.
- Send forgot-password guidance only through direct owner contact.
- Send after-test feedback after a task is completed or a user finishes testing.

## Customer Invitation

Hi [Name], I am testing AddisTask, a local services marketplace for Addis Ababa. The goal is to make it easier to post a task, compare approved providers, and manage the work in one place.

If you are willing to help test it, please create an account, post one realistic task, and tell me where the process feels confusing.

Please do not enter your exact private address in the first task description. Share exact address details only after you choose a provider.

## Provider Invitation

Hi [Name], I am testing AddisTask, a marketplace where approved local providers can find customer tasks in Addis Ababa.

If you are willing to join the pilot, please create an account, complete your provider profile, add your service area, availability, experience, and contact phone, then wait for approval before applying to tasks.

The pilot is small, so feedback is more important than volume.

## Provider Profile Reminder

Hi [Name], your AddisTask provider profile still needs a little more detail before approval.

Please check your service area, availability, contact phone, and experience details. These help customers trust who they are booking.

In Admin Mode, use Copy Reminder from the Provider Approval Queue to generate a provider-specific message with the exact missing approval items.

## Forgot Password During Pilot

Hi [Name], automated password reset is not active yet during this controlled pilot.

Please contact the AddisTask owner directly so we can verify your account and help safely. Do not send your password in group chats or public messages.

## Day-Of Test Reminder

Hi [Name], thank you for helping test AddisTask today.

Please use the app like a real customer or provider. If something is confusing, slow, crowded, or hard to find, tell me exactly where you got stuck.

## After-Test Feedback

Thank you for testing AddisTask. Could you send quick feedback on these points?

1. What was easy?
2. What was confusing?
3. Did you know what to click next?
4. Did you need to scroll too much?
5. Would you trust this flow for a real task after improvements?
